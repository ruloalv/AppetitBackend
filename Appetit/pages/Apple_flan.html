Apple flan

  • Preparation: 35 minutes
  • Cooking: 30 minutes

Flan is one of the most traditional and most popular desserts in our country. We bring you this healthy option so that you can indulge in snacks.
This delicious flan is made from apples and only contains 165 kcal.

INGREDIENTS

  • 4 apples
  • 1 glass of white wine
  • 3 eggs
  • 150 ml of milk
  • 75 g of sugar
  • 5 tablespoons of sugar
  • A few drops of lemon juice

DIRECTIONS

  1. Prepare the caramel. Put the sugar in a saucepan with 1 tablespoon of water and the lemon juice. Heat it until you get a light caramel and divide it into 6 flanrites.
  2. Peel the apples, cut them into quarters, core them and cut them into dices. Cook them with the wine, over very low heat, for 15 minutes.
  3. Drain them and wait to temper them. Reserve a part for decoration and grind the rest.
  4. Beat the eggs with the sugar and milk. Mix them with the puree apple and distribute this preparation in the flan box.
  5. Cook for 45 minutes, in a bain-marie, in the oven preheated to 180º. Let the flan cool, unmold on dessert plates and serve them with the apple cubes.

NUTRITION INFORMATION

Calories 160 calories | Total Fat 20 grams
Fiber 60 grams | Protein 45 grams
