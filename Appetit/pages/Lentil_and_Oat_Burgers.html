Lentil and Oat Burgers

  • Yield: Serves 4
  • Prep time: 20 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes

Lentil and Oat Burgers are a nutritious and delicious vegetarian option for burger lovers. This recipe serves 4 and is perfect for a hearty lunch or dinner. These burgers are packed with protein and fiber.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 cup dried green lentils
  • 2 1/2 cups water
  • 1 cup rolled oats
  • 1/2 cup finely chopped onion
  • 1/2 cup grated carrot
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 teaspoon cumin powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon chili powder
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1/4 cup breadcrumbs
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, and your favorite toppings
  • Olive oil for cooking

DIRECTIONS

  1. Rinse the dried lentils under cold water and place them in a saucepan with 2 1/2 cups of water. Bring to a boil, then reduce the heat, cover, and simmer for about 20 minutes, or until the lentils are soft and the water is absorbed. Drain any excess water.
  2. In a large mixing bowl, combine the cooked lentils, rolled oats, chopped onion, grated carrot, cilantro, minced garlic, cumin powder, chili powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. Use a blender or food processor to pulse the mixture a few times until it starts to come together but still has some texture. You want it to be sticky enough to form into patties.
  4. Transfer the mixture to a clean bowl and stir in the breadcrumbs. This will help bind the mixture together.
  5. Form the mixture into 4 burger patties. Place them on a baking sheet and refrigerate for at least 30 minutes to firm up.
  6. Heat a skillet over medium-high heat and add a bit of olive oil. Cook the burger patties for about 4-5 minutes on each side or until they are golden brown and cooked through.
  7. Toast the burger buns in the skillet or oven until they are lightly browned.
  8. Assemble your lentil and oat burgers with lettuce, tomato, and your favorite toppings. Serve hot and enjoy!

TIP:

Feel free to customize your burgers with your favorite condiments and cheese.
