Banana protein cookies

  • Preparation: 5 minutes
  • Cooking: 15 minutes

An afternoon cookie with a cup of coffee? Of course. This protein cookie contains 12 g of protein thanks to almond flour.
It is also rich in magnesium and potassium, which also makes it ideal for snacking before or after training.

INGREDIENTS

  • 1/4 of a very ripe banana
  • 4 tablespoons of almond flour
  • 1 teaspoon coconut oil or melted butter
  • cinnamon
  • 1 teaspoon of honey (optional, if we have a very sweet tooth)
  • Chocolate chips or a couple of crushed nuts.

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 200 degrees.
  2. In a bowl, mash the banana with a fork. You can use the rest of the banana.
  3. Freeze to make healthy ice cream.
  4. Add the flour, oil, cinnamon, a pinch of salt and the sweetener if you are going to use it.
  5. Form 2 small cookies and decorate with chocolate chips or nuts.
  6. Bake 15 minutes or until golden.

NUTRITION INFORMATION

Calories 250 calories | Total Fat 25 grams
Fiber 25 grams | Protein 10 grams
