Brownie healty and fit

  • Yield: serves 6
  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 20 minutes

Today's fit and healthy brownie will make you salivate, it will make you dream, it will make you run to the Cook to see if you have the ingredients... because you're going to want to prepare it now!

INGREDIENTS

  • 10' prep 15' cooking
  • 6/8 servings
  • 2 bananas
  • 1 egg
  • 2 cocoa powder without sugar
  • 3 peanut paste without sugar
  • w/n vanilla essence
  • 1 white chocolate chips
  • 1 dark chocolate chips
  • 1 walnuts or almonds.

DIRECTIONS

  1. Mash the bananas, put them in a bowl and add the egg and vanilla essence.
  2. Mix well and add the cocoa, peanut paste. Finally, add the chips and Almond nuts.
  3. Integrate everything very well.
  4. Pour into an oven-safe mold and cook for 15 minutes at 180 degrees.
  5. Remove, let cool well before unmolding
  6. Note: the I used are for dessert

Fiber 20 grams | Protein 15 grams
