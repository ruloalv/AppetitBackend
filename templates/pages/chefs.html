Chef Valentina

Meet Chef Valentina, a culinary virtuoso with an unwavering dedication to crafting dishes that embrace the essence of health and taste. Her culinary journey began as a quest to redefine traditional flavors into nourishing creations that delight the palate and uplift the spirit.
Drawing inspiration from her deep connection to organic ingredients, Chef Valentina's experience includes working alongside world-class chefs who share her commitment to responsible sourcing and sustainable practices. Her background in nutrition and culinary arts intertwines seamlessly, allowing her to curate menus that are both indulgent and health-conscious.From vibrant vegan delights to meticulously balanced protein-rich dishes, Chef Valentina's creations resonate with her belief that food is a celebration of life. Her mastery lies not only in her exquisite pairings but also in her ability to infuse each plate with a genuine passion for wellness.
As a co-founder of "Appetit", Chef Valentina invites you to partake in a culinary voyage where flavors come alive, and nourishment takes center stage. Her culinary philosophy resonates throughout the menu, offering an array of choices that honor the harmony between health and gastronomy. Discover the extraordinary touch of Chef Valentina's culinary artistry at "Appetit". Join us to relish in a symphony of flavors that embody the heart and soul of wholesome dining.

Chef Santiago

With a passion for the culinary arts that spans over two decades, Chef Santiago is a seasoned professional renowned for his innovative and flavorful creations. His journey began in prestigious kitchens across Europe, where he honed his skills under the guidance of renowned chefs, blending traditional techniques with modern gastronomy.
Having crafted delectable dishes in Michelin-starred restaurants, Chef Santiago's expertise is a fusion of artistry and precision. His commitment to using locally-sourced, seasonal ingredients results in dishes that celebrate both taste and health. From his signature Mediterranean-inspired salads to exquisitely grilled entrées, each plate showcases his dedication to crafting balanced, vibrant cuisine.
Chef Santiago's culinary prowess is not confined to the kitchen; his leadership has guided teams to deliver exceptional dining experiences. His unwavering dedication to quality and creativity has earned him a reputation as a trailblazer in the world of healthy gastronomy.
Now, as a co-founder of "Appetit" Chef Santiago invites you to embark on a journey of flavors that ignite the senses and nourish the soul. With a focus on delivering both exquisite taste and nutritional value, his dishes reflect a deep-rooted passion for culinary excellence that's evident in every bite. Join us at "Appetit" and savor the distinctive touch of Chef Santiago's culinary brilliance.
