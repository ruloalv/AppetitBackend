Healthy tres leches cake

  • Yield: servings 9
  • Preparation: 30 minutes
  • Cooking: 40 minutes

This cake is delicious, moist and with this version, you will save a lot of calories, It has less than 150 calories per serving.

INGREDIENTS

  • 150 gr oat flour
  • 4 eggs
  • 70 gr of sweetener
  • 16 g baking powder
  • Vanilla essence
  • 300 ml of skimmed milk
  • 100 ml of vegetable drink (can be almond milk)
  • 2 tablespoons of sweetener
  • 300 ml of cream or milk cream
  • Sweetener to taste
  • Cinnamon powder

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 170 degrees, you can also cook it in the air fryer.
  2. In a bowl, with the help of some electric rods if you have them, assemble the eggs with the sweetener, vanilla until the eggs double in size.
  3. Add the flour and baking powder and stir until well integrated into the dough but be careful not to lower the eggs.
  4. Put in a mold and bake at 1700C. for about 40 minutes. When the time passes baked, with the help of a fork we will scratch the surface of the cake.
  5. Now we will make the cake bath. In a container we mix both milks with the sweetener and we will add it to the cake, spreading it over the entire surface. We will let it cool in the refrigerator.
  6. Now we will whip the cream or milk cream with the sweetener and vanilla. I know it. We Will spread it on the surface and sprinkle the cinnamon powder.

NUTRITION INFORMATION

Calories 150 calories | Total Fat 25 grams
Fiber 50 grams | Protein 30 grams
