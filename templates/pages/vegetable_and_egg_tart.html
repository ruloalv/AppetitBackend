Vegetable and Egg Tart

  • Yield: Serves 6
  • Prep time: 30 minutes
  • Cook time: 40 minutes

This Vegetable and Egg Tart is a delightful dish for breakfast or brunch. It's loaded with fresh vegetables and eggs, making it a healthy and satisfying choice. Whether you're serving it for a special occasion or a regular weekend breakfast, it's sure to impress!

INGREDIENTS

  • 1 pre-made pie crust
  • 1 cup bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup zucchini, diced
  • 1 cup cherry tomatoes, halved
  • 1/2 cup red onion, chopped
  • 6 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 1/2 cup grated cheese (your choice)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

DIRECTIONS

  1. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C). Place the pre-made pie crust in a tart pan and set aside.
  2. In a skillet, sauté the bell peppers, zucchini, cherry tomatoes, and red onion until they are slightly softened.
  3. In a bowl, whisk together the eggs, milk, grated cheese, salt, and pepper.
  4. Spread the sautéed vegetables evenly in the pie crust. Pour the egg mixture over the vegetables.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven for 35-40 minutes or until the eggs are set and the tart is golden brown.
  6. Allow the tart to cool slightly before slicing and serving. Enjoy!

TIP:

Feel free to customize this tart with your favorite vegetables and cheese.
